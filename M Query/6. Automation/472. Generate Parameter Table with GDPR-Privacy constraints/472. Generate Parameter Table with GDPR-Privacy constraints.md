**Description:**

### Typical Scenario
In a real-world data pipeline, this transformation would be used when building a data mart or data warehouse that needs to serve data to various stakeholders while ensuring that sensitive information, such as personal identifiable information (PII), is protected. For instance, a company may need to provide aggregated sales data to regional managers without exposing individual customer details.
