# Filter Duplicate Records for large datasets

**Description:**
This transformation removes duplicate records from large datasets, ensuring data quality and reducing data redundancy. It is particularly useful when dealing with big data, data warehousing, and business intelligence. By eliminating duplicates, it helps in accurate analysis and reporting.

### Typical Scenario
In a real-world data pipeline, this transformation is crucial when integrating data from multiple sources. For instance, when merging customer data from different systems, duplicates may exist due to data inconsistencies or overlapping records. Removing these duplicates ensures that the analysis and reporting are based on accurate and unique data, leading to better decision-making.

`Table.Distinct`, `Table.SelectRows`
