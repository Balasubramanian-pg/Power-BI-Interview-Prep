By applying this transformation, organizations can minimize the risk of data breaches and comply with regulatory requirements. In a real-world data pipeline, this transformation would be used when handling customer data that contains sensitive information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, and social security numbers. By applying the masking transformation, the company can protect customer PII while still allowing the vendor to perform analysis on the data.
