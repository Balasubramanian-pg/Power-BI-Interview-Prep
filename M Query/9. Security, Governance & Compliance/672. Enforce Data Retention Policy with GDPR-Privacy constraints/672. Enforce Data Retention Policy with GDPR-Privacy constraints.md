# Enforce Data Retention Policy with GDPR/Privacy constraints

**Description:**
This transformation ensures that data is retained in accordance with GDPR and other privacy regulations. It helps organizations to manage data storage and deletion according to specific policies. By applying this transformation, data can be filtered and removed based on retention rules.

### Typical Scenario
In a real-world data pipeline, this transformation would be used when handling customer data that must be deleted after a certain period as per GDPR regulations. For instance, a company may need to erase customer transaction records after 6 months. This transformation can be applied in the data ingestion or processing stage to filter out data that exceeds the retention period.

`Table.SelectRows`, `Date.Year`, `Date.AddDays`
