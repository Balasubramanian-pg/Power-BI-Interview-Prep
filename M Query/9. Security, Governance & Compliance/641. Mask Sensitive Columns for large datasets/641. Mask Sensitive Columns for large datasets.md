# Mask Sensitive Columns for large datasets

**Description:**
This transformation masks sensitive information in large datasets to protect data privacy and security. It is commonly used when sharing data with external parties or for data analytics purposes where sensitive information should not be exposed. The transformation replaces sensitive values with fictional data, making it impossible to retrieve the original information.

### Typical Scenario
In a real-world data pipeline, this transformation would be used when a company needs to share customer data with a third-party vendor for marketing purposes. The company wants to protect customer personal identifiable information (PII) such as names, addresses, and social security numbers. By masking these sensitive columns, the company can ensure data privacy while still allowing the vendor to perform marketing analysis.
