# Minimize Column Scans with GDPR/Privacy constraints

**Description:**
This transformation minimizes column scans while adhering to GDPR/privacy constraints by selectively exposing only necessary data columns, thereby reducing the risk of sensitive information exposure.

### Typical Scenario
In a real-world data pipeline, this transformation is crucial when handling sensitive customer data. For instance, when a marketing team needs to analyze customer demographics without exposing personally identifiable information (PII), this transformation ensures that only non-sensitive columns are scanned and processed.
